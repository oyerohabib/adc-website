About African Democratic Congress

Who We Are?

The ADC NEWTRYBE is comprised of young individuals coming together to promote and usher in young leaders with CREDIBILITY, CHARACTER AND CAPACITY towards a transformational Nigeria. This marks the first time in the post-independence era that an idea-based political platform would have a generation committed to a new political platform to fight in the political arena of the numerous Nigeria’s political parties. TheADCNewTrybe is focused on bringing in Patriotic and purposeful Nigerians with a uniformed ideology to transcend the personality struggles of leaders and political parties.

WHAT DO WE WANT TO DO?

  • Shortlist, Screen and Select 300 Nigerian Youths within the age ranges of 25 - 40 into the House of Representatives (Green Chamber) through the #300Spartans Project.
  • Raise funds for the 300 Selected Young Leaders for their campaigns, ahead of 2023 Elections.
  • Rationale of Project: The House of Representatives currently consists of about 360 members, wherein this project will raise 300 House of Representatives Aspirants.
  • The Green Chamber is led by the Speaker and assisted by the Deputy Speaker along with eight Principal Officers that include Majority Leader, Deputy Majority Leader, Minority Leader, Deputy Minority Leader, Chief Whip, Deputy Chief Whip, Minority Whip and Deputy Minority Whip.
  • Our target is to ensure that nothing less than 150-200 of these Aspirants wins at the General Elections. Upon winning of these 200 > seats, it is expected that they would be elected by their colleagues to hold the key leadership positions in the Green Chamber.
  • We believe that if we have about 200> patriotic Nigerians in the house, we can facilitate meaningful changes, especially in the legislative arm of government through their key functions:
    • Lawmaking.
    • Correction of defects in Law/Amendment.
    • Control of Public Funds.
    • The Power of Investigation and Summons: Enforcing Accountability, Checks and Balances, Responsibility and Transparency.

Aims & Objectives

  • Raise, Build, Prepare and Promote a large (300) highly organized and formidable young leaders for the Lower Chamber of the Legislative.
  • Sponsor uniformed and collective bills at the National Assembly.
  • Center cause on pro-people, path of racial diversity and moderation guided by social policies, pro-reform and community-based movement in the political space.
  • Raising patriotic Nigerians who understand nation building, collective responsibility, accountability and transparency.
