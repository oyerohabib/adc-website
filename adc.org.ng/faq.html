Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

On ADC website www.adc.org.ng, click on JOIN ADC on the top menu of the website to get to the membership registration page. Choose category of membership that best suites you (there are two categories of membership in ADC i.e. Category 1 is for Youths/Persons with Disability while Category 2 is for Adults).

Click on the category that best suites you and complete the online form, upload your profile picture and submit.

You will be redirected to a secured online payment gateway to pay your membership registration fees. You can either choose to pay with your card or choose to pay with your Bank. Please note that all online payments on ADC website are processed by Paystack.

On successful payment, you will receive a success confirmation message – Done! Payment Received!

You can then return to the Home page, click on login to access your member’s account with ADC.

No. If you had already register online before now, you don’t need to do a fresh registration again. Kindly click on login at the top menu, ensure you reset your password by clicking on the Reset Password link on the login page. Enter the email that you used for previous registration, enter a new password and click submit to reset you password.

After successful password reset, go back to the login page. Enter your email and the new password to login to your member’s dashboard.

If you had already registered online as a member of ADC and you are visiting the new ADC website for the first time to login, kindly click on login at the top menu and reset your password by clicking on the Reset Password link on the login page. Enter the email that you used during your online registration, enter a new password and click submit to reset you password.

Click to go back to the login page, enter your email and the new password to login to your member’s dashboard.

No. You are NOT required to pay registration fees more than once.

If after making payment for registration and the fund had been deducted from your bank account but you got an error message on the website, kindly send an email to info@adc.org.ng stating your challenge with the payment process. Please note that all transactions on ADC website are processed by Paystack. If payment is successful, the registered user will be able to login to the ADC website with registered email/phone number and password. If in any case funds are deducted from the user bank account without a successful payment notification, then the issue is most likely between interswitch and the user's bank. The best we can do at this point is to get the user's email and check at our Paystack backend to confirm if the fund actually got to Paystack, if the fund had gotten to the Paystack end, the user will be contacted with member’s login details and granted access to member’s dashboard. But if the fund did not reach Paystack, the user will have to apply for refunds from his/her bank.

You will be able to pay your membership dues after successful registration as bonafide member of ADC. Login to your member’s dashboard and choose any of the monthly (N1,000), quarterly(N3,000) or yearly (N12,000) payment options.

Go to the menu and click on Where We Stand. Select Our Manifesto to view ADC Manifesto

If you are a registered member, kindly login to your member’s dashboard, check the Resources area and click on the Download button by the constitution to download the ADC constitution.

Go to the menu and click on About. Click on Party Structure on the page that appears.

At the Home page, scroll below the slider and enter the name of your state at the Search Your State Chairperson bar to find the contact details of your state chairperson.
